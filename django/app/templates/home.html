forget my ex

You have to move on! Leave the past behind, forget what happened, and enjoy your awesome future!

With forget my ex you can delete all your data on facebook related to your ex. We know that keeping that is annoying.

Remove everything that can make you remember that disgusting person that you used to know... Luckly not anymore!

But if by chance you come back dating, you can download all your data and readd to facebook.
